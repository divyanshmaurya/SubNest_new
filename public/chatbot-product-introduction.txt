How SubNest Works

1. List
Brokers, landlords, and sellers can add properties to the SubNest website.

2. Discover
Buyers and renters browse listings on the SubNest website and ask questions naturally.

3. Engage
The AI assistant responds 24/7, recommends suitable listings, and helps visitors narrow down options based on their goals, budget, and timeline.

4. Qualify and Route
SubNest captures lead details, identifies serious intent, and sends strong opportunities to the right real estate professional for follow-up.
